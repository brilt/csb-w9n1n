Politics

Finland is a democratic republic led by President Sauli Niinistö since 2012, who leads the nation's foreign policy and is the supreme commander of the Finnish Defence Forces. Its current constitution dates from 1 March 2000.

Its political system is very close to parliamentary, but the President has powers that bring the system closer to a semi-parliamentary system.

The principle of separation of powers is effective. Parliament is the legislative branch and the Council of State, headed by Prime Minister Sanna Marin (since 2019), is the executive branch. The judiciary does not depend on the executive branch.


Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences

Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences also called SeAMK (because it is called Seinäjoen ammattikorkeakoulu in finnish), is a University founded in 1995. There are currently 4700 students. The university has its own website : SeAMK.fi
